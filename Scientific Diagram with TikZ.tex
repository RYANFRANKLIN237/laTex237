\section*{Quantum State Representation}

\begin{tikzpicture}
    \draw[->] (0,0) -- (3,0) node[right] {$\ket{0}$};
    \draw[->] (0,0) -- (0,3) node[above] {$\ket{1}$};
    \draw[->, thick] (0,0) -- (2,2) node[above] {$\ket{\psi}$};
\end{tikzpicture}

The vector $\ket{\psi}$ represents a superposition of $\ket{0}$ and $\ket{1}$.
